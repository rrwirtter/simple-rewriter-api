Now that your files are on GitHub, deploy to Vercel:

1. Open new tab: https://vercel.com
2. Click "Sign Up" (use GitHub account)
3. Click "Add New" → "Project"
4. Find "simple-rewriter-api" in the list
5. Click "Import"
6. Click "Deploy" (don't change anything)
7. Wait 1-2 minutes
8. You'll see: "Congratulations! 🎉"
9. Click "Visit" to see your API

Your API URL will be:
https://simple-rewriter-api.vercel.app
